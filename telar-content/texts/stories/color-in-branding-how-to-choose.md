---
title: "How to choose colors? "
---

There are many interactive resources on the web that can help designers create a coherent palette for their brand. Adobe Color CC starts you off with a color wheel and color harmony choices: [https://color.adobe.com/create/color-wheel/](https://color.adobe.com/create/color-wheel/). Some—like Adobe and Coolers ([https://coolors.co/](https://coolors.co/)) allow you to upload a photo and create a palette by selecting colors in that photo, perhaps focusing on warm colors or bright colors. Others—like, Hail Pixel ([https://color.hailpixel.com/](https://color.hailpixel.com/))--allow you to start with one or two colors and create a palette around them, narrowing down your choices along the way to complementary, analogous, etc.
